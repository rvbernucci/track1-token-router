# Scale789 Cluster-Augmented E2B Decision

## Decision

Cluster geometry is **not promoted** into the runtime E2B regression. The current per-intent v2 matrix remains the production policy.

## Production-Compatible Evidence

The audit normalized `4,854` real scale789 Q8 assessments across legacy, expansion and boundary sources. Clustering used fit rows only; E2B correctness was excluded from geometry. Thresholds used calibration rows only, and protected rows were opened only for audit.

Direct score, compact and full-feature clustering produced candidates only for sentiment. Every direct candidate failed at least one protected distribution. The full-feature candidate selected 21 historical rows at only `76.19%` precision.

The supervised comparison added one-hot cluster membership, nearest-centroid distance, radius ratio and an outlier indicator to a regularized per-intent logistic regression. For sentiment:

| Candidate | Calibration selected | Precision | Wilson 95% lower | Brier |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|
| Compact base regression | 55 | 90.91% | 80.42% | 0.182 |
| Cluster augmented | 50 | 90.00% | 78.57% | 0.188 |

The cluster-augmented model reduced coverage, reduced precision, reduced the Wilson lower bound and worsened Brier score. No other intent produced an eligible operating point. Unknown clusters and invalid assessments remain Fireworks-safe fallbacks.

Detailed reproducible outputs are generated by `scripts/build_scale789_prediction_union.py`, `scripts/benchmark_e2b_runtime_clusters.py` and `scripts/fit_cluster_augmented_e2b.py`.
